Event at Children Homes Tengku Budriah

The programs and activities carried out are aimed at strengthening the relationship with each other

22 April 2018
UKM Year 1 Biomedical Science students have organized a visit to the Tengku Budriah Children's Home which aims to give love to the children there. They have held some interesting activities such as zumba dance, explorace and others

20 MAY 2019
In conjunction with the month of Ramadan, Raja Pemaisuri Agong, Tunku Azizah Aminah Maimunah Iskandariah with her daughter Tengku Puteri Jihan Azizah ‘Athiyatullah visited Tengku Budriah’s Children Home to see their condition. Deputy Prime Minister, Datuk Seri Wan Azizah Wan Ismail, Federal Territory Mufti, Datuk Seri Dr Zulkifli Mohamad Al-Bakri and Secretary General of the Ministry of Women, Family and Community Development, Datuk Dr Rose Lena Lazemi also attended the visit

18 September 2021
Board of Visitors Meeting No. 3 of 2021 "Thank you to YBrs Chairman/ Dato '/ Datin/ Dr/ Tuan/ Puan for the presence and beneficial partnership. May God make things easier and bless us all"
